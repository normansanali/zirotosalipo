WWE Provides Injury Update On Liv Morgan Following Attack By Rhea Ripley On RAW

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, August 5, 2024

It sounds like we won’t be seeing Liv Morgan back in the ring again anytime soon.

WWE provided a storyline update to their official website this morning, stating that “Morgan was examined by WWE medical staff before being taken to a local medical facility for further evaluation.”

It’s clear that WWE’s website update was presented in a storyline fashion. But the fact of the matter is it appears Morgan did suffer a serious injury last week on WWE RAW.

What injury is Liv Morgan suffering from?

Morgan was involved in a brawl with Rhea Ripley during the close of last week’s episode of RAW. The two women brawled over the barricade into the timekeeper’s area, where she reportedly suffered an injury.

Fan accounts noted that Morgan left RAW last week with her arm in a sling. Before RAW, Reddit wrestling insider Kerrmit125, who has gotten several stories correct in the past, reported that Morgan suffered a separated shoulder. This report has also been given credibility by both PWInsider and The Wrestling Observer.

Morgan’s shoulder injury stems from her right shoulder which is why Ripley attacked the left one last night on RAW.

A separated shoulder without surgery can take anywhere from two to 12 weeks to heal fully.
