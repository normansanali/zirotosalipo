Megan Rapinoe Net Worth 2023: How Much Is The USWNT Star Worth?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, July 26, 2024

Megan Rapinoe's journey from local talent to international superstar has been nothing short of remarkable. As one of the key players for the United States Women's National Team (USWNT), she has not only left an indelible mark on the soccer field but also on the world. As of 2023, Rapinoe's net worth stands at an impressive $5 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

Rapinoe's soccer career began to take off during her college days, where she played for the University of Portland. Her skills and dedication caught the attention of professional teams, leading her to sign with the Chicago Red Stars in 2009. Later, she moved to the Women's Professional Soccer (WPS) league and joined the Philadelphia Independence.

The pinnacle of Rapinoe's career came in 2011 when she was chosen for the USWNT. Her contributions to the team's success were significant, with standout performances in several tournaments. Most notably, Rapinoe played a crucial role in helping the USWNT secure the FIFA Women's World Cup titles in 2015 and 2019, earning the admiration of soccer fans worldwide.
