Arkansas Jordan Boone Missing Found Safe After Disapppearance

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, September 27, 2024

A significant development has occurred in the search for Jordan Boone missing case. The most recent developments clarify the issue and offer vital details about how the inquiry is progressing.

Arkansas Jordan Boone Missing

Jordan Jmon Boone, 16, had been missing since October 14, 2020, and authorities in El Dorado are aggressively looking for him. 

Jordan was last seen by his mother on a Wednesday night at about 11 pm, according to a Facebook post from the neighborhood police department. 

He was last seen standing 5 feet 3 inches tall and weighed around 119 pounds while donning a blue full-zip hooded sweatshirt and navy blue jogging trousers. 

Jordan had a telephone with limited minutes, but his account had been erased or destroyed, making it challenging for officials to contact him.

Jordan’s mother reported to the police that her kid had been asking for his driver’s license and social security card. 

Jordan is known to be a reclusive person who prefers to keep to himself. According to his mother, he only speaks on the phone with one person, whom she believes to be named “Anthony,” who he met while in a juvenile detention center.

Authorities were pleading that anyone with knowledge of Jordan’s whereabouts come forward and contact the El Dorado Police Department at (870) 881-4810 or the CrimeStoppers Hotline at (870) 863-INFO while the investigation moves forward. 

They stress that all calls will be handled privately. The El Dorado neighborhood worries about Jordan Boone’s disappearance and wants to help with the search operations.

The neighborhood police department is aggressively searching for any clues or information that could point investigators in the direction of the missing adolescent. 

The public’s assistance is crucial to the authorities’ ongoing search efforts and to ensuring Jordan’s safe return to his concerned family as they work to understand the facts behind his absence.

Jordan Boone Missing Update: Is He Found Safe? 

A week and a half after Jordan Boone was missing, a 16-year-old from El Dorado, Arkansas, has been discovered safe. 

On Thursday, the El Dorado Police Department confirmed that Jordan was in Georgia. 

The chief detective, Lieutenant Scott Harwell, appreciated everyone who shared information and helped find Jordan.

Jordan will be transferred back to his hometown of Arkansas, even if the precise date of his return has not yet been decided. Jordan has been able to call his mother since being discovered, reassuring his frightened family. 

The community and Jordan’s loved ones, who had been anxiously awaiting this good news, are greatly relieved. 

The meticulous work of the authorities and the widespread public support were instrumental in the search’s successful conclusion. The emphasis is now on ensuring Jordan’s safe return and giving him and his family the support and care they need.

Jordan Boone Family

Jordan Boone, a 16-year-old from El Dorado, Arkansas, who had been missing, was found safely, giving his family great comfort. 

The whole El Dorado Police Department, as well as the community as a whole, received heartfelt thanks from Jordan’s loved ones, who had been going through an agonizing time of uncertainty.

Overwhelmed with happiness and relief, Jordan’s family expressed their sincere gratitude for the police department’s tireless efforts in finding their kid. 

They were emotionally devastated by his disappearance, and the news of his safe return gave them immeasurable solace and thankfulness. 

They commended the police officers’ devotion, professionalism, and dedication in their tireless efforts to protect Jordan.

Jordan’s family expressed not only thankfulness but also deep appreciation to the entire neighborhood for their continuous support during the search. 

The worry, prayers, and support received from family, friends, neighbors, and even total strangers was evidence of the resilience and generosity of the El Dorado neighborhood. 

The family realized their combined efforts were crucial to the case’s successful conclusion.
