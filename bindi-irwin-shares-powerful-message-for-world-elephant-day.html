Chandler Powell shares message of support for wife Bindi Irwin as she takes social media break

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, August 1, 2024

Chandler Powell has thrown his support behind his wife, Bindi Irwin, after she announced that she’s taking a break from social media.

In a post to Instagram shared on Monday, the 24-year-old wrote:  ‘You inspire me with your unwavering kindness and your ability to stand up for what is right. 

‘You are the strongest and most beautiful person inside and out. Grace has an amazing mama to look up to’. 

Support: Chandler Powell has thrown his support behind his wife, Bindi Irwin, after she announced that she’s taking a break from social media. Pictured with their baby girl Grace 

It comes as Bindi reveals she is taking a break from public life, after she shared a scathing Facebook post about her grandfather, Bob Irwin, on Sunday.

In an Instagram post on Monday, the Wildlife Warrior, 22, shared a black and white photograph in which she cuddled her three-month-old baby daughter Grace, and said she was ‘working hard to protect her mental health’.

She wrote at length in the caption: ‘Hi guys, Just a note to say thank you for your support. I’m taking a break from social media and most of my work in the public eye for a month to be with my beautiful daughter and my wonderful family.

She also took to Facebook to repost a photo of husband Chandler Powell, 24, cuddling their daughter Grace to mark Father’s Day in the USA. 

The posts come after Bindi sensationally spoke out about her strained relationship with her estranged grandfather Bob Irwin Snr in a scathing Facebook comment.  

On Sunday, she shared a sweet post celebrating Father’s Day in the USA, saying she was thankful for the ‘three most incredible fathers in her life’; her late father Steve Irwin, her husband Chandler and her father-in-law Chris.

She went on to claim that Bob had ‘returned gifts I’ve sent after he opened them’, and ignored any letters sent from her. 

‘From the time I was a little girl he has ignored me, preferring to spend time doing anything else rather than being with me,’ she continued.
