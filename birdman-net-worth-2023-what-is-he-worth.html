About Birdman’s Life and Career

Birdman, born in the lively city of New Orleans, Louisiana, in 1969, initially made his mark as a rapper in the early 1990s. However, his ambitions extended beyond performing. Teaming up with his brother, Ronald “Slim” Williams, Birdman co-founded Cash Money Records in 1991, signaling a pivotal moment in his career and propelling him to unparalleled recognition in the music industry. Cash Money Records gained renown for nurturing and showcasing exceptional talents like Lil Wayne, Drake, and Nicki Minaj, solidifying Birdman’s reputation as an innovative and visionary music executive.

Ronald “Slim” Williams’s Net Worth

Apart from his role as Birdman’s sibling, Ronald “Slim” Williams commands a significant presence in the music realm. His partnership with Birdman in Cash Money Records has contributed to his accumulation of a net worth reaching $10 million by 2023. Additionally, his solo album, “So Many Stars,” launched in 2009, showcased collaborations with renowned artists such as Birdman, Lil Wayne, and Drake.
