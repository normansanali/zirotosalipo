Brian Fichera and His Wife Of Eight Years, Dylan Dreyer Share Two Sons. Also, Expecting a Baby Numbe

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, July 17, 2024

A writer and producer, Brian Fichera, 32, and his wife, Dylan Dreyer, 39, a meteorologist are a powerful couple in the media industry. Both of them have had an incredible profession that has made them popular across the world. Brian is an NBC News cameraman who is known for his work as a guest in the news talk show, Today.

Whereas Dylan, a native of New Jersey is a renowned journalist who is known for working with NBC News since September 2012. Before that, she has worked for Weekend Today, MSNBC, Today, n The Weather Channel and NBC Nightly News.

Apart from that, let's discuss further details regarding their married life. 

Brian and Dylan Are Married Since 2012

For the first time, the duo met while they working the early shift at the Boston based WHDH television station, Seven News in Boston, as per Good Housekeeping. She was working as an anchor and meteorologist and he was a studio technician.

After working together more, Fichera and Dreyer started hanging out in the Weather Center with their old friend named Todd. And the three of them build a true friendship and become closer. Eventually, Fichera proposed to his girlfriend Dylan on the front of the porch of his parent's home with a ring in July of 2011.

On October 6 2012, the pair exchanged the wedding vows at the Cathedral of the Holy Cross in Boston. Their nuptials took place in the presence of their entire family and friends. Not long after getting married, the newlywed's duo shifted to New York City from Boston. Then Dreyer's career kicked off with Today. 

As of today, Dreyer, the co-host of 3rd Hour Today and her eight-year-husband Brians share a happy and healthy bonding with no issues. In fact, they share a lot of other interests and frequently post family photos on their social media accounts.

Brian and Dylan's Three Children

From their blissful marriage, the husband and wife are blessed with three adorable kids, all of them are sons. At first, they announced that they were welcoming their first child together in June 2016. On 18th December 2016, their oldest son, Calvin Bradley Fichera was born at 11:52 am. 

He weighed 8.3 lbs, 3 oz, and was 20.5 long. Similarly, their second son, Oliver Bradley Fichera, was born on 3rd January 2020 after Dylan struggled with fertility issues. After her baby's birth, she stated that she just feel so happy and so blessed.

Recently on May 11, 2021, the Today co-anchor again surprised her fans revealing on the air that she and her husband are expecting their third child. By sharing the family photo together with holding the sonogram on her Instagram account", she wrote, "You see cal when two people are isolated together for over a year".
