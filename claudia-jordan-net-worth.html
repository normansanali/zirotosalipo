Claudia Jordan Wiki Biography

Claudia Jordan is a widely recognized American actress, radio and television personality, as well as a model. How rich is Claudia Jordan? According to sources, Claudia Jordan’s net worth is estimated to be $500,000. The majority of her wealth and net worth Claudia Jordan has gathered due to her active career on the radio, as well as television. Born in 1973, in Providence, Rhode Island, Claudia Jordan started her career by participating in beauty pageants held in Rhode Island. Jordan was fairly successful at that, as she won both the Miss Rhode Island Teen USA title in 1990, as well as the Miss Rhode Island USA title 1997. These pageants opened more opportunities for Jordan in the entertainment industry, as she strived to be not only a model, but also an actress.

Jordan’s initial acting career consists mainly of appearances on music videos. In 1996, she was featured on Ginuwine’s music video for a song “Only When You’re Lonely”, and later worked with a famous boy band Backstreet Boys and appeared on their video for “As Long As You Love Me”. Some of Jordan’s other early music video appearances include Chico DeBarge’s “Listen To Your Man”, Joe’s “I Wanna Know”, as well as Little Richard’s videos. Even though Claudia Jordan managed to collect some money from her video appearances, as well as increase her net worth, she did not stop at that. In 1999, Claudia Jordan starred in an environmental documentary series titled “Trippin’”, with Cameron Diaz as the host of the show. Some of other celebrities who were guests on the series include Mark Hoppus, Justin Timberlake, Jessica Alba and Drew Barrymore. Jordan continued making appearances on television screens with the comedy-drama series “Jack & Jill” where she co-starred with Ivan Sergei and Amanda Peet.

However, she is perhaps mostly recognized for modeling in an international game show called “Deal or No Deal”. Jordan modeled a #1 briefcase for five seasons of the show. The revenue made from “Deal or No Deal” contributed a decent amount of money to Claudia Jordan’s net worth. Prior to appearing on “Deal or No Deal”, Jordan worked for two seasons on another game show series called “The Price Is Right”. With her rising popularity, Claudia Jordan started receiving more requests to be featured on various television series and news programs. Soon enough Jordan was given a chance to host a Super Bowl Piece on a famous news show “Entertainment Tonight”. Jordan has also been a guest host on “Access Hollywood” and “Extra”, hosted the pilot of “The Fantasy Show ESPN”, and co-hosted “Ballers”. In addition to that, Claudia Jordan starred in “Celebrity Apprentice”, “That’s So Raven” with Raven Symone, “One on One”, as well as “Dr. Phil” with Phil McGraw. Claudia Jordan is a well-known television personality and an actress who has an estimated net worth of $500,000. Jordan is currently hosting a radio show titled “The Claudia Jordan Show”, and co-hosts a talk show called “Tiny’s Tonight”, where her co-hosts are Tamar Braxton and Trina Braxton.
