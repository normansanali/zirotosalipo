Who Is DeShanna Marie Minuto? Her Bio, Age, Jillian Michaels, Husband

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, August 24, 2024

DeShanna Marie Minuto was popular as the girlfriend of The Biggest Loser host and the fitness trainer, Jillian Michaels. But in September 2021, the two stepped up their game and became fiancees.

This DeShanna Marie Minuto Bio explores everything you need to know about Jillian’s partner. 

Meet DeShanna Marie Minuto, Jillian Michaels Partner

DeShanna and Jillian officially started dating on October 7, 2018 and went on to get engaged after 3 anniversaries. On their third anniversary, Jillian took it to her IG to write, “We are all asleep until we fall in love” Happy Anniversary @deshannamarie. I adore you.”

The news about their engagement came after DeShanna flaunted her big diamond ring over her IG, captioned, “YES!”. Intrigued about the ring? Well, it was a 7-carat emerald-cut diamond featuring two single-carat, trapezoid diamonds on each side. And it was crafted by a family friend at H&H Jewels in Miami. 

“DeShanna is all the things”, Jillian shared about her future spouse — “beautiful, smart, funny, creative, quirky, passionate … I could run down the list of adjectives,” she explained.

Also, Jillian went on to add that her girlfriend has revealed herself to be “strong beyond measure. wise beyond her years, and loving beyond (her) wildest dreams.”

Overall, the pair were a match made in heaven. They supported each other, and admittedly taught each other something new every day. Furthermore, considering one’s a trainer and the other’s a D-1 athlete, the duo even worked out together.

What’s more, DeShanna was even challenging Jillian with the cardio exercises. “She’s like I ran my mile in 7 minutes and 15 seconds. Baby are u on a 6/5? I’m like, Mind your business,” Jillian joked about her fiancee.

Did DeShanna Marie Minuto Have A Husband In The Past?

No, DeShanna Marie Minuto didn’t seem to have had a husband in the past. Though it wasn’t clear, we believe that her engagement with Jillian was her first.

As for Jillian, DeShanna’s her second fiancee. She was engaged to the talent manager and yoga instructor, Heidi Rhoades in the past. The two were a longtime couple who got engaged during an episode of the E! reality show Just Jillian in 2016.

It was Jillian who popped the question to her ex-fiancee in a private movie theater. “I know I’m an ass —- a lot of the time, but if you will continue to put up with me, I would like you to marry me,” Jillian recalled her words.

The duo shared two children — a daughter named Lukensia (whom Jillian adopted from Haiti in 2012) and a son named Phoenix (whom Heidi gave birth to in the same month.) They were both 9 as of 2021.

DeShanna Marie Minuto Career

DeShanna Marie Minuto is the owner of the luxury fashion brand “Letterino“. Reportedly, she founded the company in 2019, after working as a creative director at several NY Agencies, helping to revamp and revitalize brands across all categories of business.

According to her company’s webpage, DeShanna had a successful career but she was never “completely fulfilled.” Thus, she took her long-time love of fashion and mixed it with modern history, and began recreating family heirlooms into works of art.  Initially, she started with her idols and transformed her grandfather’s WWII Bomber and her father’s 1960’s biker jacket.

Soon, the brand exploded.

As of 2021, the brand sold Motorcycle club jackets, Aviator jackets, Bomber jackets, Military souvenir jackets, and Denim and Varsity jackets.

Even Jillian has one flaunted a Letterino bespoke jacket on the red carpet for the Toy Story 4 premiere.

When DeShanna wasn’t working, she loved snowboarding, riding horses, and traveling.
