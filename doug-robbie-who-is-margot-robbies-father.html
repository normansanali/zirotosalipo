Who is Margot Robbie's father?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, July 31, 2024

Margot Elise Robbie is an Australian actress and producer who was born in  Dalby, Queensland on July 2, 1990, to her parents –  Sarie Kessler and Doug Robbie.

She is from a family of four siblings and happens to be the second youngest. Her older siblings are Anya and Lachlan and her younger brother is Cameron.

Robbie’s first acting roles came during high school when she starred in two low-budget independent thriller films, called Vigilante and I.C.U., both released years later.

Robbie made her television debut in a 2008 guest role as Caitlin Brentford in the drama series City Homicide.

Doug Robbie: Who is Margot Robbie’s father?

Doug Robbie is a celebrity father who is the father of actress Margot Robbie.

Doug Robbie is a former farm owner and sugarcane tycoon.

He was married to Sarie Kessler – Margot’s mother for several years but separated when Margot was five.

His children have minimal contact with him since they were raised by their single mother after they parted ways.
