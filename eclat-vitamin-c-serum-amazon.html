The Eclat Vitamin C Serum Has 7,000 Five-Star Ratings on Amazon

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, July 17, 2024

Amazon shoppers have quite an affinity for vitamin C serums, with some of the site's best-selling options having upwards of 31,000 five-star ratings. So it comes as no surprise that when customers discovered an affordable one with promising results, thousands of reviews poured in.

The Eclat Organic Vitamin C Serum landed on Amazon in September (according to price tracking website CamelCamelCamel), and it's already gained over 7,000 five-star ratings. The $7 serum contains 20 percent vitamin C, along with other ingredients like hyaluronic acid, ferulic acid, jojoba oil, and vitamin E. The brand says their vitamin C is cold pressed, which means no heat or chemicals were used to extract it.

Customers reveal that the serum has helped with dry skin, hyperpigmentation, wrinkles, and dullness.
