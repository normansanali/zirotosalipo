Emily Maynard Calls Mormon Fiance Jef Holm a &quot;Bore-Mon&quot;

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, September 17, 2024

The TV cameras are off, but they're still performing!

Nearly three weeks after Us Weekly exposed Bachelorette Emily Maynard's cheating scandal, the ABC reality star and her fiance, Jef Holm, made a special effort to pack on the PDA in front of photographers and fans at a Thursday Fashion's Night Out event at New York City's Macy's flagship store.

As Bethenny Frankel, Scott Disick, Michael Kors and others mingled nearby, Maynard, 26, and her fiance, 28, browsed the Benefit Cosmetics offerings hand-in-hand and snuck kisses in between taking photos with fans.

Though they put on a united front Thursday, the couple's relationship is on anything but solid ground. "The things they are doing right now are to save face, because the whole world knows they are having problems," a source tells Us Weekly of the stay-at-home mom and her water company exec fiance, who got engaged during The Bachelorette's July finale.
