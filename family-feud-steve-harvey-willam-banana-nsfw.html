Celebrity Family Feud fans nearly die over Steve Harveys reaction to contestant taking biza

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, July 27, 2024

CELEBRITY Family Feud has gotten a little too bananas for Steve Harvey and some fans' liking on July 17's episode.

The host looked petrified when drag performer Willam Belli pulled a banana from her wig before making an X-rated guess.

On last Sunday’s Family Feud, drag queen Willam left Steve, 66, literally without words.

Willam was called up to the podium to square off against Neil Patrick Harris' husband David Burtka.

Willam- the A Star Is Born actor and only contestant ever disqualified from RuPaul's Drag Race- pulled a banana out of her hair while at the buzzer.

Willam then took a bite out of the fruit before passing it to competitor David, who did the same.

Willam then offered the banana to a stunned Steve, who turned down the offer to have a bite.

“You thought it was a banana in my wig, but I was just happy to see you,” Willam suggestively told the host.

FANS GO BANANAS

Some viewers claimed the beloved game show had gone too far, while others were not so bothered.

“Steve Harvey’s face when the queen pulled a banana out of her hair!” tweeted one viewer.

“As I’m watching The Show and Saw William pull out a Banana out of her hair and yeah I agree this episode has gone Bananas,” wrote another fan.

“Steve was NOT ready for @willam and the banana,” added another.

"Omg when Willow pulled the Banana out of her hair I thought Steve was gonna die I laughed so hard bout made myself sick lol" wrote a fourth.

'DID SHE JUST SAY...'

The stars of the reality series Drag Me To Dinner, including Neil and his husband David, faced off against a team of drag queens.

Things only got more NSFW from there, as Willam and Burtka faced a very raunchy category.

The two stars were asked to name “Something That Married People Do That a Dog Might Do While Making Love.”

Willam buzzed in with an X-rated answer that was bleeped on the TV broadcast.

Again, Steve looked astonished while Burtka and the in-studio audience laughed.

“Did she just say eat a**? #CelebrityFamilyFeud” asked one viewer on Twitter.

“I wanna know what she actually said to where they had to censor it…” wrote another fan.

"@willam what did you say on Family Feud that got bleeped?!" wondered a third.

Another fan blasted: "This was a family show?"

To which someone else defended: "Ummm it hasn't been for a long time.

The questions are always super suggestive and the answers are dirty. Plus this is really not that crazy."

“They come up with these risqué questions so don’t be surprised when contestants say inappropriate things,” defended one more viewer.
