Freddie Mercury aka Farrokh Bulsara was a British record producer, songwriter, singer, lead vocalist of Queen and rock band. As of 1991, Freddie Mercury’s net worth is $30 million. He was in the industry from the year 1969 to the year 1991. He was also known by the names Larry Lurex, and Freddie Bulsara.

He majorly worked with instruments, keyboards, and vocals, famous for the genre rock, and worked with the labels Hollywood, Parlophone, EMI, Polydor, and Columbia. He was a legendary singer in the history of rock music, he was known for his flamboyant stage persona and four-octave vocal range.

Freddie Mercury Wiki/Biography

Born on 5 September 1946, Freddie Mercury’s age was 45 Years Old 1991 at the time of his death. He was born and brought up in a middle-class Parsi family from Stone Town, Tanzania. He holds an British nationality and has his belief in Zoroastrian religion. His zodiac sign is Virgo and ethnicity is Indian Parsi.

Freddie Mercury Childhood

He completed his early education at the Boarding School , Panchgani, Maharashtra, India. After that, he also enrolled himself at the Isleworth Polytechnic College in Isleworth, England and later at Ealing Art College from where he completed his graduation. From the very beginning of his childhood, he was very much interested in co-curricular activities rather than in studying.
