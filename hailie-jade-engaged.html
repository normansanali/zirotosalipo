Hailie Scott Mathers Engaged: All About Her Fianc Evan McClintock

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, August 21, 2024

One of the most popular (and divisive) rappers of all time is Eminem. According to Britannica, he has given his fans a ton of autobiographical rap songs about his life since his 1996 debut. These songs paint frank accounts of his troubled marriage to his ex-wife Kim Scott and the strained bond he has with his mother, Debbie Nelson. Among the deep dives and (sometimes uncomfortable) revelations are the songs he wrote for his daughter, Hailie Jade, which often took on a brighter tone. According to People, Jade was mentioned in songs such as “My Dad’s Gone Crazy,” “The Way I Am,” and Dejà Vu,” and nearly two dozen other tracks.

Jade is making major strides right now! The native of Detroit earned a psychology degree from Michigan State University. She also graduated with a 3.9 GPA, as her father revealed during an appearance on “Hot Boxin’ with Mike Tyson”; she majored in psychology. Jade seems to have changed her career since then, transitioning into a podcast host and social media influencer. Jade has nearly five million followers across TikTok and Instagram, where she frequently shares fashion and beauty-related content.

Oh, and did we also mention that Evan McClintock and Jade are engaged? Jade shared the wonderful news on Instagram, but who exactly is the love interest of Eminem’s daughter?

Evan McClintock works in brand protection

According to his LinkedIn profile, Evan McClintock is an executive in charge of business expansion who provides “complex loss investigatory technology and services to enterprises and large government entities.”

McClintock continued, “My most lucrative use cases land me in the FMCG [fast-moving consumer goods] industry. McClintock is currently employed by Scout, a software development firm based in Royal Oak, Michigan, as reported by People. The business specializes in case management software, protection for high-end brands, anti-counterfeit measures, and software for intellectual property.

McClintock attended Michigan State University, much like Hailie Jade did, however he earned his bachelor’s in economics two years sooner, in 2018. Prior to that, he studied business and marketing at Rochester College. Before starting his professional career, McClintock was a driver for UPS. McClintock’s net worth, according to the journal, might range from $1 million to $5 million. And even though, according to Wealthy Gorilla, that’s not even close to Eminem’s projected $230 million fortune, it’s still nothing to laugh at!

Evan has been with Hailie since they were in college

Despite having a sizable social media following, Hailie Jade hasn’t divulged a lot of information regarding her relationship with Evan McClintock. There isn’t much information available regarding McClintock’s life outside of work due to his scant social media presence, which consists of a private Instagram account. Having saying that, the couple hasn’t entirely slipped under the radar. For instance, it seems that McClintock and Jade have been together for at least six years. McClintock initially appeared on Jade’s Instagram back in 2016, according to PopSugar. In the Instagram post, Jade gushed, “Couldn’t have asked for a better 21st birthday celebration (or a nicer person to have by my side).

McClintock has occasionally shown up on Jade’s feed throughout the years, but she has shown herself to be quite private about their relationship. In light of this, their recent engagement probably signals the beginning of several months of amusing glimpses at their wedding preparations, bachelor and bachelorette parties, and of course, the big event: their upcoming nuptials. Jade can flaunt her stunning engagement ring in the meanwhile, which Nicki Swift just said is probably worth at least $100,000.
