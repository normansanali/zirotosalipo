Heardle 90s: A Fun and Nostalgic Music Game

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, August 16, 2024

Heardle 90s is a daily music game that challenges you to guess the correct 90s song based on a short snippet of the intro.

It is inspired by Wordle and Heardle but focuses on songs from the 90s.

Heardle 90s music game has drawn a lot of players worldwide due to its simplicity and nostalgia.

In this article, we will tell you everything you need to know about Heardle 90s, such as how to play, where to find it, and what other versions are available.

How to play Heardle 90s

Playing Heardle 90s is very easy and fun.

All you need to do is visit website 1 and click on the play button at the bottom of the screen.

You will hear a short clip of the intro of a 90s song, and you have to type in the name of the song or the artist in the box below.

If your answer is correct, you will see a green check mark and your score will increase.

If your answer is wrong, you will see a red cross mark and more of the intro will be revealed.

You can also skip your turn if you have no clue, but this will also unlock more of the intro.

The goal is to guess the song in as few tries as possible and share your score with your friends.

Where to find Heardle 90s

Heardle 90s is available on the website, which is part of the Heardle Decades family of games.

You can also access it from the main website, where you can find other versions of Heardle based on different decades, genres, and artists.

You can also follow Heardle Decades on Twitter @heardledecades for updates and news.
