Is Luis Gerardo Mndez Gay? Everything To Know About His Dating Life

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, August 9, 2024

Many people are raising questions about whether the Mexican actor and producer, Luis Gerardo Méndez, is gay or straight. Let’s explore Luis’s dating life in this article.

Luis Gerardo Méndez rose to prominence after playing in The Noble Family, a 2013 comedy film that became Mexico’s highest-grossing picture for a few months.

In 2013, Méndez appeared in the film Cantinflas, and as of 2015, he is co-producing and co-starring in Club de Cuervos, Netflix’s first-ever original Spanish production.

Is Luis Gerardo Méndez Gay? 

According to Luis Gerardo Méndez’s Wikipedia profile, he was questioned about his sexuality during an interview in which he used a masculine phrase to allude to his previous relationships.

He has been in a relationship with Pablo Chemor since January 2016 and can be seen posting pictures of them together on Instagram.

More detailed information regarding Luis’s sexuality might be revealed soon by himself in the upcoming days.

Meet His Partner Or Husband: Their Dating Life

Luis Gerardo Méndez is currently in a relationship with Pablo Chemor.

Pablo began learning music at the age of four in Mexico City, where he studied the piano and traditional Mexican music. 

His partner, Pablo, eventually traveled to London, where he studied at King’s College for his bachelor’s and master’s degrees. 

After returning to Mexico City, Pablo began writing and producing music for films and then for the stage.

Luis Gerardo Méndez Family

Luis Gerardo Méndez was born to Mexican parents in Aguascalientes City, Mexico. However, their parents’ and siblings’ names are still yet to be disclosed publicly.

Méndez has a puppy named Tuba, and the two of them starred in a PETA ad encouraging people to love their dogs like family members.

Find Them On Instagram

Luis Gerardo Méndez has registered his Instagram account under the username @luisgerardom.

He has a verified account with more than 2.6 million followers on Instagram. Luis has posted 1,548 posts on Instagram and seems to be active often.

His partner, Pablo, can also be seen using an Instagram account under the username @pachemor. He does not have many followers like Luis, but he does have 4,903 followers.

More About Luis Gerardo Méndez

Luis Gerardo Méndez was nominated for an Ariel Award for Best Actor for his portrayal of Javi Noble in The Noble Family.

For his portrayal in Cantinflas, he received a New York Latin ACE Award for Best Supporting Actor in a Motion Picture.

In 2019, Méndez was nominated for a second Ariel Award for Best Actor in a Leading Role for his performance in Bayonet.

His production business, Cine Vaquero, just signed a first-look agreement with ViacomCBS International Studios.
