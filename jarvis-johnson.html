Early Life and Childhood

Jarvis Johnson was born on May 5, 1992, and he is currently 30 years old. He is originally from the United States and his zodiac sign is Taurus. He has not shared any information regarding his parents, siblings, and other family details.

Career and Professional Life

Jarvis is a Youtuber who is also a software engineer. More precisely, in September 2014, he became a software engineer at Yelp, which is a business rating website, and then a senior software engineer in July 2016. He then became an engineering manager in April 2018 at Patreon, which is a service for funding online creators.

Similarly, he is also a social media star who is famous for his self-titled YouTube channel. He has gained massive popularity for his vlogging about his life as a software engineer alongside challenges and tech commentary.
