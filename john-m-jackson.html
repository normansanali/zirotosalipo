John M Jackson

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, July 14, 2024

John M. Jackson is a well-known American actor. He is most recognized for his role as Rear Admiral A.J. Chegwidden in the CBS series JAG. From 2017 to 2018, he portrayed A.J. Chegwidden on NCIS: Los Angeles.

What is John M Jackson’s Net Worth?

John M Jackson has a net worth of $500 thousand USD and a salary ranging from $19,647 to $210,569 USD. He played Judge Edward Nichols in the film, which grossed $43 million dollars at the box office.

John M. Jackson’s Biography, Age, and Education

John M Jackson  was born on June 1, 1950, in Baton Rouge, Louisiana, USA. He is currently 69 years old. He grew up in Fort Worth, Texas. In terms of education, John graduated from Arlington Heights High School in Ford in 1968. He was a football and basketball player in high school. Later, in Austin, Texas, he attended the University of Texas at Austin.

Professionalism, Teaching, and Acting

He was a teacher before becoming an actor, and he taught social studies at LBJ High School in Austin, Texas. He moved to New York City and then to Hollywood in 1980. Similarly, he played minor roles in a number of TV and film productions. In the 1980s, he appeared in films such as Back Roads, Murder in Texas, American Playhouse, Local Hero, Find My Child, License to Kill, Celebrity, Not My Kid, Right to Kill? The Legend of Billie Jean, Crime of Innocence, The Hitcher, Stillwatch, In Self Defense, and others.

He later acted in films such as Chinatown Connection, Sudie and Simpson, Eve of Destruction, Line of Fire: The Morris Dees Story, Love, Lies, and Murder, Career Opportunities, Switched at Birth, Shannon’s Deal, Deadly Matrimony, A Few Good Men, and many more. He’s lately been on NCIS: Los Angeles, Gifted, Stalker, Rizzoli & Isles, Criminal Minds, NCIS: Naval Criminal Investigation Service, Perception, Private Practice, Castle, CSI: Crime Scene Investigation, Knight Rider, Grey’s Anatomy, and other shows.

Relationship, Married, Wife

John M. Jackson is married. Jana Gale Hawkins is his wife. They married on October 13, 1979. Conor Jackson was born on May 7, 1982, in Austin, Texas. Katherine is their daughter. Before making his Major League debut with the Arizona Diamondbacks, he was a First Baseman in the Diamondbacks minor league system.
