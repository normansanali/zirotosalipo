Julia Garner - Bio, Career, Age, Net Worth, Height, Facts

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, August 5, 2024

Julia Garner is a famous American actress. Julia Garner is most popular for her starring role in the Netflix crime drama series Ozark, for which she received critical acclaim and won three Primetime Emmy Awards.

Early Life and Childhood

Julia Garner was born on February 1, 1994. Her birthplace is in The Bronx, New York, U.S.A. She is currently 29 years old. Garner’s horoscope is Aquarius.

Further, her mother is Tami Gingold who is a therapist who had a successful career as an actress and comedian in her native Israel. Likewise, her father Thomas Garner is a painter and an art teacher. Garner is Jewish.

Moreover, she has an older sister named Anna Garner who is an artist. This personality attended Eagle Hill School in Greenwich, Connecticut. Hence, she began taking acting lessons at age 15 to overcome her shyness. Garner graduated with a degree in psychology from Indiana University in 2015.

Julia Garner – Net Worth 2024

This personality has earned a huge amount of money from her successful career as an actress. Her net worth is around $3 million.

Career

Julia Garner is a famous American actress who is best known for her starring role as Ruth Langmore in the Netflix crime drama series Ozark, for which she received critical acclaim and won three Primetime Emmy Awards for Outstanding Supporting Actress in a Drama Series in 2019, 2020, and 2022.

Moreover, she also had roles in the FX drama series The Americans, the Netflix miniseries Maniac, and the Bravo true crime series Dirty John. Likewise, in 2022, she portrayed Anna Sorokin in the Netflix miniseries Inventing Anna, for which she received nominations for a Golden Globe and another Primetime Emmy Award.

Furthermore, in films, she has starred in Electrick Children, We Are What We Are, Grandma, and The Assistant, as well as having appeared in The Perks of Being a Wallflower, Martha Marcy May Marlene, and Sin City: A Dame to Kill For.

Talking about her relationship status, she is married. Garner married Mark Foster who is a singer and lead vocalist of Foster the People. The couple married in December 2019 in a ceremony at the New York City Hall, eight months after they got engaged.
