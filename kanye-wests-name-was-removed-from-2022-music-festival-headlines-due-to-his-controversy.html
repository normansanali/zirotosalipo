Kanye Wests Name Was Removed From 2022 Music Festival Headlines Due To His Controversy

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, August 18, 2024

Kanye West’s recent scandal has prompted his opponents to criticize Coachella for deleting his name off the festival’s 2022 lineup. An online user using the handle “Kim Pete” has started a change.org petition, claiming that organizers should be embarrassed for providing West with “any more platform” to spew hatred at his ex-wife, Kim Kardashian, and her current partner, Pete Davidson. The petition, which had gathered over 1,650 signatures by March 17 afternoon, said that no one had stood up to West until now, and those who have been publicly chastised by the rapper.

“For over a year, we have seen Kanye harass, manipulate, and harm Kim, Pete, and others. This is absurd that he is permitted to do so freely.”

The petition was launched on the same day that Instagram suspended West, 44, for 24 hours for continuously attacking Kardashian, 41, and Davidson, 28, as well as comedians D.L. Hughley and Trevor Noah. The petition on Change.org presently has 5,118 supporters.

What is Kanye West’s relationship with Kim Kardashian and Pete Davidson?

West has accused Kardashian of using her four children to antagonize and gaslight him, citing daughter North’s usage of TikTok, and said that he had become more estranged from his family. He’s also been cyberbullied for weeks, decapitating a cartoon portrayal of Davidson in his new music video, Eazy.

Rather than caving into West’s demands, Kardashian has pledged to take the “high road” in their pending divorce. Despite having formally separated in March 2022, the ex-couple is still wrangling over child custody and property concerns. Kardashian was seen in a sneak peek film published on YouTube by The Ellen Show, speaking with presenter Ellen DeGeneres on the next show, which will run on April 13. When DeGeneres inquired as to why Kim has always been protective of Kanye West despite the rapper’s continual abuse, the SKIMS founder responded:

“I’m always going to be protective. I’ve always wanted my children to see only the finest. As difficult as it might be at times, I strive to ignore it and do what is best for the children.”

She continued by praising her mother, Kris Jenner, and ex-husband and late father of the Kardashians, Robert Kardashian, for providing a positive example for her. Additionally, the social media sensation said that regardless of what happens, Kanye is the father of her children. Coachella 2022 will take place in Indio, California, between April 15, 2022, and April 17, 2022, and again between April 22, 2022, and April 24, 2022. Kanye West, Harry Styles, and Billie Eilish will headline.
