Kelli Giddish is an American television, stage, as well as a film actress. Since 2011, she has played NYPD Detective Amanda Rollins in the NBC crime-drama television series “Law & Order: Special Victims Unit“. Previously, she played Di Henry on the ABC soap opera “All My Children” (2005–2007), Dr. Kate McGinn in the Fox crime-drama series “Past Life” (2010), and Annie Nolan Frost in the NBC crime-drama series “Chase” (2010–2011). In 2005, she made her theatrical film debut in the horror movie, Witches of the Caribbean.
