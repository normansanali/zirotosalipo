Kylie Jenner and Her Baby Bump Reportedly Appear in a New Video

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, September 15, 2024

Kylie Jenner was spotted out and about for the first time since September (when her pregnancy was first reported), TMZ reports. A new photo and video of the beauty mogul captured yesterday shows her visiting a construction site in Hidden Hills with her friend Jordyn Woods and mom, Kris Jenner.

Kylie is dressed in all black, with an oversized shirt and leggings, but her baby bump appears to show in the footage. Watch the full video here.

Jenner is reportedly having a baby girl with Travis Scott, but neither she nor the rapper have publicly confirmed her pregnancy yet. E! reported yesterday that the 20-year-old is due in early February, just a few months before it's speculated that her older sister Khloé Kardashian will give birth, in April.

This isn't the only time Kylie has added fuel to her pregnancy rumors this week. On Monday, she appeared in new Calvin Klein ads with her sisters, but covered up her stomach in every photo. The 20-year-old wrapped up in a blanket or posed behind her sisters to hide her midsection in the images.

The Kardashian-Jenner clan hasn't confirmed whether the poses were intentional—they've yet to confirm whether the pregnancy rumors are true at all—but it sure looks suspicious.

Willy Vanderperre

Jenner hasn't publicly spoken out about her pregnancy yet, but she's apparently "counting down the days" to welcome her baby, a source told E!. Though she's "nervous about giving birth," Kylie's in good company with her mother, who reportedly helped set up a nursery, and her older sisters, who are imparting their advice on motherhood, the insider added.
