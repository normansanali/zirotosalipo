Lesa Milan's Mina Roe Website Crashes After 'RHODubai' Debut

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, August 28, 2024

Lesa Milan was seen debuting her fashion brand Mina Roe’s new Ready-to-Wear collection at 2022’s Arab Fashion Week Spring/Summer on last week’s episode of The Real Housewives of Dubai. And, as fans flooded to her official website to get their hands on her new duds, the site crashed not once, but twice.

According to a new report, Lesa’s website couldn’t keep up with the over 20,000 people who attempted to visit amid the Bravo reality series’ June 15 episode, which featured Lesa showcasing her luxury maternity fashion brand’s post-pregnancy clothing line.

As Inquisitr shared, Lesa was joined during her show by co-star Chanel Ayan and other models from her agency as other castmates, including Caroline Brooks, Nina Ali, and Sara Al Madani, sat in the front row. Meanwhile, the last remaining cast member, Caroline Stanbury, was a no-show with some thinking her then-upcoming wedding to husband Sergio Carrallo had gotten in the way and others being more suspicious of her absence.

“We couldn’t think of being anywhere else but here tonight,” Brooks noted during the episode. “You know we’re all here, and I feel so bad that Caroline is not here.”

Nina also weighed in on Stanbury’s absence, saying that she believed “the wedding stuff has her like overwhelmed” as Chanel expressed doubt.

While Mina Roe specializes in maternity options, Lesa’s newest collection was created for women who have already given birth, but who weren’t yet back to their pre-baby size.

Over the years, Lesa has grown her brand into a celebrity favorite. And, as Inquisitr confirmed, it is popular with the likes of Beyonce, Serena Williams, Khloe Kardashian, Gigi Hadid, Katy Perry, and Jen Lilley.

The Real Housewives of Dubai season one airs Wednesdays at 9/8c on Bravo.
