What Is Linda Evangelistas Net Worth?

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, August 5, 2024

What Is Linda Evangelista’s Net Worth?: Linda Evangelista is a Canadian fashion model.

She developed a passion for modeling at a younger age and at 12 attended a self-improvement course in a modeling school where she was taught things such as poise and etiquette.

Evangelista has been consistent throughout her career and has risen to become one of the most sought-after fashion models.

She’s regarded as one of the most accomplished and influential models of all time and has been featured on over 700 magazine covers

Evangelista started her modeling career in 1984 when she signed with Elite Model Management in New York City.

She had her hair cut short in 1988 upon the suggestion of photographer Peter Lindbergh. The haircut was then nicknamed “The Linda”.

“The Linda” sparked many copies worldwide and also benefited Evangelista’s career and helped usher in the era of the supermodel.

Described as the “chameleon” of the fashion industry, she was one of the most famous women in the world during the late 1980s and throughout the 1990s.

Evangelista retired from her career in 1998 and made a comeback three years later, this time working only sporadically.

Her achievements as a model led to her being voted “The Greatest Supermodel of All Time” by the viewers of the television show Fashion File in 2008.

Evangelista is known for being the longtime “muse” of photographer Steven Meisel, as well as for the phrase: “We don’t wake up for less than $10,000 a day.

What Is Linda Evangelista’s Net Worth?

As of August 2023, Linda Evangelista has an estimated net worth of about $50 million. She has been featured on many magazines’ cover pages worldwide and has also featured over 700 magazine cover pages during her career.
