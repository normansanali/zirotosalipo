Mikala Jones Children: Meet Bella Jones And Violet Jones

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, August 10, 2024

Mikala Jones, a professional surfer known for his adventurous journeys to discover undiscovered surfing locations, was killed while riding the waves off Indonesia’s Mentawai Islands.

The surfing world mourns the passing of this daring spirit whose passion for the sport was unparalleled.

Who are the children of Mikala Jones?

Mikala Jones, who was born on Hawaii’s Rocky Point, was destined for a life of surfing.

He made a huge influence in the surfing world alongside his brothers, Malia, Keoni, and Daniel Jones. His bold attitude and exceptional talent enabled him to leave a lasting impact.

Mikala’s two children, Bella and Violet Jones, are among many who have been severely touched by his terrible death.

Mikala’s eldest daughter, Isabella Lokelani, expressed her great sadness and thanks for the essential life lessons her father provided on her Instagram account.

While the surfing world mourns the death of a real great, Mikala Jones’ achievements will continue to inspire future surfers.

His incredible voyage, filmed in spectacular films and images, will be eternally carved in surfing history.
