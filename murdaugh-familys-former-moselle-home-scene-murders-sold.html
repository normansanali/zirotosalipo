Murdaugh familys former Moselle home, scene of murders sold WSOC TV

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, August 19, 2024

COLLETON COUNTY, S.C. — The Murdaugh’s former family home has been sold to a new owner, according to Crosby Land Company.

Commonly referred to as “Moselle” or the “Moselle Farm,” the more than 1,700-acre property sold for $2.6 million, according to a deed.

The buyers are listed as James A. Ayer and Jeffrey L. Godley on a real estate title.
