Nenu Student Sir Will Have 3 Mind-blowing Twists

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, September 4, 2024

Producer Satish Varma won accolades for his maiden production venture Naandhi and his second movie Nenu Student Sir with Ganesh is also a different one. The producer says like in Naandhi, there is a new point in Nenu Student Sir as well.

“The movie falls under the thriller genre. Bellamkonda Ganesh fits this story well. I think, student sir, the initial craze will continue. The movie will definitely live up to the expectations set by Naandhi. Ganesh is a perfect choice.”

Though the story was penned by Krishna Chaitanya, Rakhi Uppalapati directed it. “Krishna Chaitanya narrated the story for half an hour. The story begins with a mobile phone. It is something that connects everyone. There are three mind-blowing twists. Krishna Chaitanya was unable to direct the film since he committed to do another project. Rakhi told me another story. But that’s not in my zone. I liked the way he handled a web series he made earlier.”

The producer affirms there’s no special message in the movie. “We have shown students’ life in the university. It includes a couple of incidents that we read in the paper. We usually ignore them. But it will be revealed by this student. It will be interesting to know about this incident when watching the movie.”

Satish Varma believes that the movie will appeal to the audience of all age groups.
