New Merch Roundup: Miraidon and Koraidon accessories at the Pokmon Center, Pok Peace plush brace

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, August 14, 2024
The Pokémon Center has announced their latest merch collection, Treasured Way Home, featuring Scarlet and Violet mascots Koraidon and Miraidon, which will be available on March 23rd in-store at Pokémon Centers in Japan. Merchandise like t-shirts, Nintendo Switch carrying cases, and stationary will feature the two Pokémon in their Ultimate Mode and Apex Build forms. Some of the more unique pieces from the Treasured Way Home collection include an LED framed art of Miraidon and Koraidon for ¥4,950 ($33.20 USD), a glasses case with a cleaning cloth for ¥2,420 ($16.23 USD), and even kids’ sport sandals for ¥7,480 ($50.18 USD). Pieces will be available for pre-order at the Pokémon Center Online beginning March 21st,
Pokémon fans in Japan have even more merchandise coming their way, this time from Takara Tomy Arts, a subsidiary of toy company Takara Tomy, which has a very long history collaborating with the Pokémon brand. Their Poké Peace collection, which includes a variety of plush products featuring Pikachu, Piplup, Scorbunny, Rowlet, Espurr, and Pichu, is seeing the addition of plush sherpa snap bracelets. The plush on the bracelets is between 4 and 10 cm (~1.5 to 4 in) tall and each comes in at ¥1,760 ($11.81 USD). The new Poké Peace snap bracelets will be available via Takara Tomy’s website, only shipping within Japan, beginning March 28th. Build- A-Bear has announced some news for international fans, debuting the latest addition to their Pokémon line, Bidoof. Standing 8 inches tall, Bidoof is the fourth Generation IV Pokémon to come to Build-A-Bear, following Piplup, Leafeon, and Glaceon. Bidoof is available now in Build-A-Bear stores for $36 USD, or as an exclusive online bundle which includes a plaid shirt, Poké Ball print bandana, and a voice chip containing 5 Bidoof sounds for $63 USD.
