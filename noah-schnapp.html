Noah Schnapp Career Milestones

Noah Schnapp’s career took off in 2015 when he made his debut in the film industry with ‘Bridge of Spies,’ directed by Steven Spielberg. This marked the beginning of a journey that included diverse roles, such as voicing Charlie Brown in ‘The Peanuts Movie’ and its video game adaptation.

As his career blossomed, Schnapp ventured into voice acting in the animated film ‘The Legends of Hallowaiian’ (2018) and secured recurring roles in the series ‘Liza On Demand.’ He also appeared in music videos like ‘In My Feelings’ by Drake.

The year 2020 marked a significant period for Schnapp as he starred in three movies – ‘Abe,’ ‘Waiting for Anya,’ and ‘Hubie Halloween.’ Additionally, he featured in the music video ‘See You’ by Johnny Orlando.
