How to swim and collect underwater items

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, July 28, 2024

If you are someone who was damaged by the water in Returnal several times before you understood that maybe you aren’t ready to get them yet. While those Siplhium healing items taunt players underwater, the good part is they won’t be away from players forever.

How to get Underwater Items

To get your hands on those underwater items in the game, players will be required to get an ability known as Hadal Ballasts. They are unlocked when players beat the fifth biome – Fractured Wasted. Fortunately, players can’t miss getting the Hadal Ballasts, as their unlock is part of the story like the Atropian Blade and Icarian Grapnel. Selene will stumble upon a corpse and will add them to her suit, as was the case with previous power-ups.

Once players get the Hadal Ballasts, all they need to do is jump into these pools of water and they will be able to sink to the bottom, enabling them to pick items that they wanted for so long. To pick these items, players need to simply press Triangle when they are standing by them, just as they would any other item.

When players are done swimming and picking all underwater items in the game, they will have to press X to jump back out of the water and continue on with their adventure. That’s everything players need to know on swimming and getting underwater items in the game.
