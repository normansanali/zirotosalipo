Rey Mysterio Age, Height, Weight

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, September 18, 2024

Rey Mysterio Age, Height, Weight: Rey Mysterio, officially known as Oscar Gutierrez Rubio is an American professional wrestler born on December 11, 1974.

He developed a love for wrestling at a younger age and has been consistent throughout his career rising through the ranks to become one of the most sought-after wrestlers.

He’s currently signed to WWE, where he performs on the SmackDown brand and is regarded as one of the greatest professional wrestlers of all time.

Rey Mysterio kicked off his professional wrestling career in 1989, at age 14, on the independent circuit, before signing with Asistencia Asesoría Administración (AAA) in 1992.

After performing for Extreme Championship Wrestling (ECW) for a while, Mysterio departed to World Championship Wrestling (WCW) in 1996.

Under the WCW, Mysterio helped popularize lucha libre in the United States, which led to the rise of cruiserweight wrestling divisions where he won the WCW Cruiserweight Championship five times and the WCW World Tag Team Championship three times.

Rey Mysterio then wrestled for Consejo Mundial de Lucha Libre (CMLL) and later joined World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE) in 2002.

Under WWE, he won the Cruiserweight Championship three times, the World Heavyweight Championship, the Intercontinental Championship, and the United States Championship, all of them which he won twice, the WWE Championship once, and the Tag Team Championship four times

In 2005, he left WWE and appeared in New Japan Pro-Wrestling (NJPW) and AAA, before returning to WWE in 2018, winning the U.S. Championship twice and SmackDown Tag Team Championship once with his son, Dominik.

Mysterio has held 24 championships between WWE and WCW (including three world championships). In addition, he is WWE’s 21st Triple Crown and Grand Slam champion.

In March 2023, Rey Mysterio made headlines when it was announced that, he will be inducted into the WWE Hall of Fame (class of 2023).

This year’s ceremony will be held following SmackDown on March 31, 2023, in Los Angeles, California.

Rey Mysterio Age

Rey Mysterio celebrated his 48th birthday on December 11, 2022. He was born on December 11, 1974, in Chula Vista, California, United States. Mysterio will be 49 in December this year.

Rey Mysterio Height & Weight

Rey Mysterio stands at a height of 1.68m and weighs around 79 kg
