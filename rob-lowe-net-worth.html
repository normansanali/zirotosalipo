Rob Lowe | Career

Rob Lowe was born Robert Helper Lowe on March 17, 1964, in Charlottesville, Virginia, United States.

His father was a trial lawyer, and his mother was a teacher.

Rob was raised in Dayton, Ohio, alongside his younger brother Chad Lowe and was baptized into the Episcopal Church.

He attended Oakwood Junior High School.

However, his parents divorced, and Rob, his brother, and mother, moved to the Point Dume area of Malibu, California.

He then attended Santa Monica High School.

Lowe’s big break came in 1979 with the TV series A New Kind of Family.
