Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, July 31, 2024

A talented teenage football player, Ryan Keeler died unexpectedly, leaving many startled and seeking explanations. According to a recent official declaration from the Clark County Coroner’s office, “Ryan Keeler’s cause of death” was natural. He died of “cardiac dysrhythmia due to hypertrophic cardiomyopathy.”
