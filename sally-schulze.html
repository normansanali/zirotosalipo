Sally Schulze Journalism Career

Over the years, Sally Schulze has engaged in various positions in the journalism field. As per her Linkedin account, the lady started her career as a general assignment reporter at WICD-TV in Champaign, Illinois, in April 1994. She also worked as a documentary producer and broadcast teaching assistant at the University of Illinois.

Moreover, she served as a weeknight anchor at WDFE-TV in Chattanooga. She then worked as a weekend anchor at KIRO-7-TV in Greater Seattle for three years and also spent some years as a morning anchor and investigative reporter at WESH-TV in Orlando.

Schulze worked as a news writer for FOX 32 before becoming an anchor. Later she attended as Weekend Morning News Anchor and reporter in June 2017, where she primarily anchored Saturday and Sunday morning news from 7 a.m. to 9 a.m.
