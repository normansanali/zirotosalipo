Serena Williams Works Booty in Swimsuit, Tubes With Colton Haynes

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, July 12, 2024

What a trio! Taking some time off from the court, Serena Williams spent the past weekend tubing down the San Marco River in Texas with a female friend and Arrow hunk Colton Haynes.

As seen in an Instagram shot Williams shared on Monday, Oct. 5, the 21-time Grand Slam winner, 34, flaunted her curvaceous body in a tangerine-hued, printed one-piece swimsuit, teamed with flat sandals, during the riverside adventure.

Meanwhile, the Arrow alum, 27, who also starred in Teen Wolf's early seasons, worked his ripped physique in a pair of navy-blue board shorts. Williams' media rep, Val Vogt, appeared in the shot, too, clad in a Baywatch-worthy red one-piece. And of course, each one held a tube in hand.

Making sure to shout-out her weekend companions, Williams affectionately wrote, "Core memories with @valgabond @coltonlhaynes love you guys."
