Shirtless Theo James Runs Away From Police in Brand New Time Travelers Wife Trailer Wat

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, August 7, 2024

A shirtless Theo James runs away from police in the brand new trailer for HBO’s The Time Traveler’s Wife.

The 37-year-old actor stars opposite Rose Leslie in the upcoming series, and in the new trailer, tell their own sides of their unusual romance.

Desmin Borges and Natasha Lopez will also star in the show, which is described as an “intricate and magical love story”.

“This is a story of loss but it’s not a tragedy. It’s about a time traveler but it’s not science fiction,” executive producer Steven Moffat previously said of the show. “Despite Clare and Henry’s time-scrambled relationship, this is the tale of a grand, ordinary love — but not necessarily in that order.”

The Time Traveler’s Wife will premiere this Spring on HBO.
