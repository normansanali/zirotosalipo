Biography

Tony Dorsett was born in Rochester, PA on April 7, 1954. Standout University of Pittsburgh football player who became a Hall of Fame running back with the Dallas Cowboys. He won the 1976 Heisman Trophy and helped lead the Cowboys to victory in Super Bowl XII. He and Tim Tebow finished their careers near the top of the list of most rushing touchdowns in NCAA history. At Hopewell High School in Western PA, he won the running back battle after catching a 75-yard screen pass.
