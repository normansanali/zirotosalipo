We’re talking about when the toe post flies out of the footbed, the sole rips or when we can feel our heel hitting the ground — when we need to replace old pairs because they’re broken. Wouldn’t it be nice to just find a flip flop we love and be able to trust that it’ll last more than one summer, max?

The Tory Burch Thin Flip Flop is a game-changer in the sandal world. The ease, the comfort and the style are all there, but the problems have — poof — disappeared! We expect a lot from a top designer like Tory Burch, and even our expectations were surpassed when we saw this sandal!

This flip flop’s upper, lining, footbed and outsole are all made of rubber.
