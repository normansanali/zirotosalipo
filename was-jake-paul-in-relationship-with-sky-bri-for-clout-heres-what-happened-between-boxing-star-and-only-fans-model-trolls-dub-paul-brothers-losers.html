Was Jake Paul in relationship with Sky Bri for clout? Here's what happened between boxing star and O

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, July 27, 2024

CLEVELAND, OHIO: Jake Joseph Paul, popularly known as Jake Paul, is a well-known internet personality and professional boxer. He recently made headlines for being featured on the cover of Sports Illustrated. Moreover, he has generated significant anticipation by confirming his upcoming match with professional boxer Nate Diaz, scheduled for August 5, 2023.

This article focuses on his former girlfriend, Sky Bri, who is an OnlyFans model and Instagram influencer. Recently, there have been discussions about the boxer's relationship with Sky Bri after his breakup with his long-term girlfriend Julia Rose. Let's explore whether Paul's involvement with Bri was solely for publicity or clout.

‘This is for clout’

After Paul's on-and-off relationship with Rose came to an end in March 2021, he swiftly entered into a brief romantic encounter with Bri. According to Bri, her involvement with Paul was primarily driven by his desire for revenge against Rose. Supposedly, Paul had made his intentions clear from the start and didn't mind Bri gaining some attention from the situation. 

During a recent appearance on the 'No Jumper' podcast, Bri shared the following statement, “The very first night, the first conversation we had, ‘This is for clout.’ He was like, ‘At the end of this, I want you to post it.” Further, she shared, "Get the clout from it whatever, and I’m probably going to make Julia mad and get the revenge that I need.” Bri revealed that Paul explicitly stated their involvement was solely to provoke his ex-girlfriend Julia. Also, she stated that Paul's intentions were to incite jealousy and anger in Julia as a form of revenge.
