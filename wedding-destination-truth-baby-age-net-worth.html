Who is Hallie Gnatovich?

If you are into mystery, the paranormal, and other intriguing facts or legends, then you have certainly been introduced toh Josh Gates. He gained his fame exploring the paranormal around the world, showcased in the reality series “Destination Truth”, and others which he has co-hosted with his wife, Hallie Gnatovich. So, what do you know about his wife?

Hallie was born on 26 January 1981 in the USA, but further information about her birth date and place remain unknown to the media. She is a former actress, and now a counselor with her own company Hallie G Therapy, however, she came to prominence after becoming the wife of the famous producer and TV reality star Josh Gates.

So, do you want to find out more about Hallie, from her early life to most recent career endeavors and her personal life as well? If yes, then stay with us for a while as we will bring you closer to Hallie Gnatovich, the wife of Josh Gates.

Hallie Gnatovich Wiki: Early Life, Family, and Education

Hallie has remained silent about her childhood years, and hasn’t shared any information in regards to it. So for now, the names and professions of her parents, then whether she has any siblings, or even which high school she went to, remain unknown. However, we know that she enrolled at Oberlin College, from which she obtained a Bachelor of Arts degree in Theater in 200, and four years later she became a student of the Alliant International University, from which she received a Master’s degree in Marriage and Family Therapy/Counseling in 2010, and two years later became a licensed counselor.

Career Beginnings

With a degree in theater, Hallie started looking for her acting debut; in 2007 she finally secured her first role, when she was chosen for the part of Marnie in the short film “Archer House”, in which the lead roles were given to Riley Rose Critchlow, Tawny Bell, and the award-winning actress Troian Bellisario. This role turned out to be her first and only acting engagement, as since then, Hallie has focused on other ventures.

Josh Gates was born on the 10th August 1977, in Manchester-by-the-Sea, Massachusetts USA, and is an author, television producer and television host, who came to prominence after appearing in the reality show “Destination Truth”. Information about his life before fame remains shrouded in mystery, though it is known that he enrolled at Tufts University.

One of his first appearances on screen was in the reality TV show “Beg, Borrow, & Deal”, which kick-started his career. It was in 2006 that he got exposure through the Budweiser series “Truly Famous”, and then the following year was chosen in the main cast for the show “Destination Truth”. Since then, Josh has worked on a number of projects, including “Ghost Hunters”, “Fact or Faked: Paranormal”, and “Stranded”, among many others. He is currently working on another show that deals with paranormal, entitled “Expedition Unknown”.
