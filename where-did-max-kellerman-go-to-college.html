Where did Max Kellerman go to College?

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, July 8, 2024

Max Kellerman, a prominent figure in the sports broadcasting industry, embarked on a fascinating journey that began with his academic pursuits at prestigious institutions. Let's delve into the details of Max Kellerman's college experience and the impact it had on his career.

Early Life and Education

Max Kellerman was born on August 6, 1973, in the Bronx, New York, and grew up in the Greenwich Village neighborhood of Manhattan. His early education took place at P.S. 41 and Hunter College High School, where he excelled academically.

College Education

After completing his high school education, Max Kellerman pursued his higher education at Columbia University, where he majored in history and graduated in 1998. During his time at Columbia University, Kellerman's passion for sports and media continued to thrive, leading him to pursue a career in sports broadcasting. His academic journey at Columbia University provided him with a strong foundation for his future endeavors in the entertainment industry.

Broadcasting Career

Impact on Career and Personal Development

The intellectual environment and diverse experiences at Columbia University provided Max Kellerman with the tools to navigate the complex landscape of sports media.
