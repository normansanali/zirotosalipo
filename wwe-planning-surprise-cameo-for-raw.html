WWE Planning Surprise Cameo For RAW

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, September 7, 2024

WWE brought a big surprise for fans on SmackDown, as The Rock made his way back for one-night appearance. It appears that surprise cameos might become a trend.

WWE has a lot of people around that they might be able to get in for a one-night cameo. It wouldn’t be a big surprise to see an unexpected name on RAW next week.

BWE posted behind their X account to drop a rather cryptic note. The bona fide WWE insider said that fans should expect to see “The Dragon” on RAW this week. No further information was given.

“All eyes on the dragon for RAW cameo.”

We’d like to know exactly who The Dragon is, but we’ll find out soon enough when RAW goes live. If anything, this will be quite an unexpected moment for RAW.

WWE has Dragon Lee i NXT, and Ricky “The Dragon” Steamboat just proved he’s able to appear on pro wrestling television after his recent AEW cameos. Only time will tell which dragon shows up on RAW, but it appears that something cool is coming this Monday.
