Yevgeny Milkovich

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, July 12, 2024

Yevgeny Milkovich is a fictional character in the show Shameless. Shameless is an American comedy-drama television series. It first aried on January 9, 2011. Shameless was adapted from a long-running, award-winning British television drama of the same name. The show is set in the South Side of Chicago .The series is mostly filmed in the North Lawndale community area of Chicago and Burbank, California, at Warner Bros. Studios.

The series depicts the poor, dysfunctional family of Frank Gallagher. Frank is a neglectful single father of six. As of December 20, 2020, 125 episodes of Shameless have aired and is currently in its eleventh season. The show is rated 8.6 in IMDB, 81% in Rotten Tomatoes, 8.8 in TV.com and 91% Google users liked the show.

Yevgeny is the son of Svetlana Yevgenivna, formerly known as Svetlana Milkovich or Svetlana Fisher who was a main character of Shameless and Mickey Milkovich who is known as one of the most aggressive neighborhood thugs in the show. Yevgeny Milkovich was introduced in season 4.
